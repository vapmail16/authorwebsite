Vikkas Arun Pareek

Vikkas Arun Pareek is a British-Indian author with five books to his credit. His latest book, Letters to My Mother, has crossed 5,000 copies sold in its first year.

He is a practicing numerologist, tarot reader, and Vastu consultant, as well as a Reiki Grand Master. He lives in London with his family.

Books

Letters to My Mother

An epistolary drama fiction, which delves into the far-reaching consequences of one's actions and the power of redemption and unity in the face of adversity. Published in Aug 2023 by Vishwakarma Publishers.

Voices of the Silent Creek

A drama fiction. The story set in rural India takes us through unimaginable yet common atrocities which a woman faces in today's society. Republished in 2024 by Kitaablovers.

The Adventures of Blue Flamingo Series

Book I: A collection of illustrated short stories for children, meant to inspire little ones to be kind, brave and informed about right choices in life, inculcating strong fundamentals.

Book II: Second book in the series dedicated to Swach Bharat Abhiyaan.

Personalized Edition: A special version where the reader can be part of the story by writing their name and pasting their picture to make the story personalized for the kid reading the book.

Achievements

  • "Letters to My Mother" has sold over 5000 copies in its first year since release.
  • The Adventures of Blue Flamingo Book 1 and 2 have been placed in Rajasthan school libraries across state (600 copies).
